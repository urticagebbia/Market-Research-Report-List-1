<p><p>The metalworking cutting fluids market comprises several types designed to enhance machining performance. Neat oil cutting fluids, composed of pure oil, provide excellent lubrication for heavy-duty operations. Emulsion cutting fluids mix oil and water, offering effective cooling and lubrication for various applications. Semi-synthetic fluids combine both properties, delivering superior cooling and lubrication with reduced oil content. Synthesis fluids, derived from chemical processes, provide optimal performance and environmental benefits, making them suitable for advanced machining techniques.</p></p>
