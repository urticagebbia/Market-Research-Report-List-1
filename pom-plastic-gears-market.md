<p><p>The POM plastic gears market includes various types, each serving distinct applications. Helical gears feature angled teeth, allowing for smoother and quieter operation, making them ideal for high-speed applications. Worm gears, consisting of a screw mechanism, provide high torque and reduced speed, suitable for applications needing significant force. Together, these gear types cater to diverse industrial requirements, enhancing performance and reliability.</p></p>
