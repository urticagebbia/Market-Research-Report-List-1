<p><ul><li>Inherent life jackets</li><li>Inflatable life jackets</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The life jacket market comprises primarily two types: inherent life jackets and inflatable life jackets. Inherent life jackets are made from buoyant materials that provide floatation without the need for inflation; they are durable and reliable, suitable for various water activities. In contrast, inflatable life jackets are lightweight and compact, offering greater comfort and ease of wear, as they deploy automatically or manually when needed. Both types serve critical safety functions, catering to diverse user preferences and conditions.</p></p>
