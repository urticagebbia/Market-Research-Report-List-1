<p><p>The rock climbing safety equipment market includes various essential types, each designed to enhance climber safety. Climbing harnesses support climbers by distributing weight and arresting falls. Climbing helmets protect against head injuries from falling debris. Passive protection, like nuts and cams, secures the climbing route by anchoring to rock features. Belay devices manage rope slack during climbs, ensuring controlled descents and falls. Climbing carabiners, used for connecting gear and anchoring the rope, are vital for secure and efficient climbing.</p></p>
