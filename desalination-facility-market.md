<p><strong>The Desalination Facility Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Reverse Osmosis (RO)</li><li>Multi-Stage Flash (MSF)</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The desalination facility market encompasses various technologies to convert seawater into fresh water. Reverse Osmosis (RO) utilizes semi-permeable membranes to separate salt and impurities from water, making it highly efficient and popular. Multi-Stage Flash (MSF) employs thermal processes, heating seawater and rapidly flashing it into steam, which is then condensed to produce fresh water. Other methods include Electrodialysis and Vapor Compression. Each technology offers distinct advantages and is chosen based on factors like energy efficiency, cost, and local water needs.</p></p>
