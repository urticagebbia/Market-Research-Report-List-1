<p><p>The whey protein products market consists of three main types: concentrate, isolate, and hydrolysate. Whey protein concentrate contains a lower percentage of protein (typically 70-80%) and retains beneficial nutrients. Whey protein isolate is processed to remove fat and lactose, resulting in a higher protein content (90% or more), ideal for those seeking pure protein sources. Whey protein hydrolysate is pre-digested for faster absorption, making it suitable for athletes and post-workout recovery, as it reduces recovery time and muscle soreness.</p></p>
