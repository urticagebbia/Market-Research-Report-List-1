<p><strong>The Fluorometers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Filter Fluorometer</li><li>Spectrofluorometer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The fluorometers market comprises two primary types: filter fluorometers and spectrofluorometers. Filter fluorometers utilize optical filters to isolate specific wavelengths of emitted light, making them ideal for routine measurements in applications like environmental monitoring and clinical diagnostics. In contrast, spectrofluorometers provide detailed spectral information by employing diffraction gratings or prisms, allowing for precise analysis of complex samples in research and pharmaceutical settings. Both types cater to diverse market needs, leveraging unique advantages for various scientific applications.</p></p>
