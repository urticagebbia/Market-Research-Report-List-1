<p><strong>The Pneumatic Nailer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Coil-Style Pneumatic Nailer</li><li>Strip-Style Pneumatic Nailer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The pneumatic nailer market is primarily divided into coil-style and strip-style nailers, each catering to different application needs. Coil-style pneumatic nailers hold a larger quantity of nails in a circular configuration, allowing for continuous nailing with less frequent reloads, ideal for tasks like roofing and siding. Conversely, strip-style nailers utilize nails arranged in a linear strip, providing precise placement and control, making them suited for framing and finishing work. Each type optimizes efficiency and performance for specific construction tasks.</p></p>
